Our Story

A Sanctuary of
Culinary Heritage

Nestled within Hotel White Lotus on Maruthi Veethika Road, Udupi, Madhuram Restaurant brings together the finest flavors of North and South India under one roof. From crisp dosas to rich North Indian curries, every dish is a celebration of authentic tradition — crafted with care, served with warmth.

The interiors, adorned with breathtaking mythological murals, transport our guests to a world where art and gastronomy meet — creating an experience that lingers long after the last bite.
