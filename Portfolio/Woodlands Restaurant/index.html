Our Story

A Tradition of Pure Taste

Nestled in the heart of Udupi, Woodlands Restaurant has been a cherished destination for those who seek authentic, wholesome vegetarian cuisine. Our kitchen honours time-tested recipes — from crispy South Indian dosas to luscious North Indian curries — all prepared fresh, every day.

With warm wooden interiors, attentive service and a menu that spans snacks, thalis, Indo-Chinese and indulgent desserts, every visit feels like a celebration.
