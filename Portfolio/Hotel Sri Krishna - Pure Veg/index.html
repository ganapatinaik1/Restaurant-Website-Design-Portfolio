Our Story

Rooted in
Devotion & Taste

Nestled in the heart of Udupi's Badagu Pete Road, Hotel Sri Krishna has been the city's cherished pure vegetarian destination. With two floors — a vibrant ground floor and a serene AC fine-dining level — we offer an experience that blends heritage, warmth, and exquisite cuisine.

🌿
100% Pure Vegetarian Every ingredient, every dish — strictly pure veg with no compromises.
