Our Story

A Legacy of
Pure Vegetarian
Cuisine

Nestled in the heart of Manipal, Krishna Kuteera is more than a restaurant — it is an experience. From the ornate entrance adorned with a majestic Krishna statue to the warmly lit art-deco interiors, every corner reflects our deep reverence for Indian heritage and flavour.

Our chefs craft every dish with the finest seasonal ingredients, honouring traditional recipes while embracing the diversity of Indian culinary art — from crisp South Indian breakfasts to hearty North Indian thalis.
