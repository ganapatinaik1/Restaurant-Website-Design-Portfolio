Our Story

Rooted in Coastal Flavours

Naadu — meaning "homeland" in Kannada — was born from a simple desire: to bring the vibrant, spice-kissed flavours of coastal Karnataka to every table. Nestled in the heart of Mangaluru, we blend age-old recipes with a contemporary dining experience.

From ragi mudde served on banana leaf to delicate cheesecakes that whisper of home, our menu is a love letter to the land. Every dish is prepared with locally sourced ingredients and cooked with the reverence the cuisine deserves.
